Easy Way to Start Vote Pre-Registration
Pre registration is one of the best ways to enforce your opinions and vote as fast as possible, as easily as possible. A voter must be registered 15 days before the election date making last minute registrations impractical and often leads to people forgetting to register before their 1st opportunity to vote. Pre-registration acts as a quick and easy way to finish your registration as soon as possible, with little to no added hassle.

Political Spectrum Test
Take a few minutes out of your day to find your political stance. Acting as a great way of gauging one’s personal political stance, the political compass test is a quick questionnaire regarding a multitude of different types of issues and your own stance on them. The test is a great way to learn which parties your personal beliefs may align with, even without a strong understanding about different policies.
Which candidates or government officials you stand with?
Are you looking to find out which candidates or government officials you stand with? Take the ISideWith quiz, a quick questionnaire regarding different policies or ideas and your own personal stance on it. The quiz provides a list of all of the important figures within the government which best shares your outlook on certain issues so that you can better support the candidates who can help you.

Pre-registration Process:
Upon beginning the pre-registration process, you will need to select the “Pre-register to vote: for California residents age 16 or 17 who want to be future voters” option. Once the pre-registration process is in progress, you will be asked a few questions about your qualifications, personal information, identification, home and mailing address, and political party preference. If you would like to opt-out of choosing a political party preference, you will be able to do so. The next section of the application includes personal preferences and consent. You will be answering a few questions about how you want to receive your voter information, volunteer opportunities, and accessibility. At the end of this section, you will be affirming that everything you have filled out is true. After this, a page will pop up for you to confirm, review, and edit all the information that you have submitted. Once you have reviewed everything for any mistakes, you are all set and should be pre-registered to vote!
